How it works

Four steps, phone only.

  1. 01

    Attach & describe

    Drop in files and a simple natural-language prompt from your phone.

  2. 02

    Review the plan

    The API generates an ordered file list, assumptions, steps, and estimated cost. Reorder or refine before approving.

  3. 03

    Approve

    On approval, the plan is enqueued as a worker job on Azure Container Apps. Nothing expensive runs without your sign-off.

  4. 04

    Poll & download

    The app polls every few seconds. When the worker finishes, tap Download to open the finished artifact.
